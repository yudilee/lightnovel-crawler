# Privacy Policy
**Lightnovel-Crawler**

_Last updated: 2025-12-16_

---

## 1. Overview
Lightnovel-Crawler (“the Service”) is an open-source application which includes both an web application and a python package.

The web application allows registered users to provide URLs to online novels, fetch text and images from those sources, and generate downloadable eBooks.

This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected, how it is stored, and how it is used in the web application.

---

## 2. Data Controller
- **Owner:** Anonymous 
- **Contact:** lncrawl@pm.me  
- **Jurisdiction:** Not bound to any specific country  

---

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account Information
The following data is required to create and maintain a user account:
- Email address  
- Username  
- Password  

Passwords are **never stored in plain text** and are securely hashed using the **Argon2id** algorithm.

---

### 3.2 Content Data
Based on user requests, the Service stores:
- Crawled novel text
- Crawled novel images
- Generated eBooks and related metadata

All content is stored **permanently** in a PostgreSQL database hosted on servers located in the **EU region (Hetzner)**.

---

## 4. Data We Do Not Collect
The Service explicitly does **not** collect, store, or process:
- IP addresses
- Access timestamps
- Device or browser information
- Usage logs
- Cookies
- Analytics or tracking data
- Third-party telemetry

---

## 5. Cookies
The Service uses **no cookies**, including:
- No session cookies
- No preference cookies
- No tracking cookies

---

## 6. Analytics
The Service does **not** use any analytics software, whether first-party or third-party.

---

## 7. Authentication & Security
- Authentication is password-based only
- Passwords are hashed using **Argon2id**
- No OAuth or third-party authentication providers are used
- Reasonable technical measures are applied to protect stored data

---

## 8. Data Sharing
User data is **not sold, rented, or shared** with third parties.

The Service may expose a public API; however, user account data is not publicly accessible.

---

## 9. Adult Content
The Service may store and process adult content if explicitly requested by users.  
Users are solely responsible for the content they choose to access and generate.

---

## 10. Donations & Payments
- The Service is free to use
- Optional donations may be made via **PayPal**
- No payment or billing information is stored by the Service

---

## 11. Data Retention
- Account data and generated content are stored indefinitely
- Users may request account deletion by contacting **lncrawl@pm.me**
- Deletion may result in permanent loss of all associated data

Depending on applicable laws, users may request:
- Access to stored personal data
- Deletion of their account

Requests must be sent to **lncrawl@pm.me**.
